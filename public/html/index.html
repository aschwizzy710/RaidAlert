About Raid Alert

In the past weeks, there have been aggressive immigration raids all across the United States causing an immigration crisis.

Reports all across the country tell us that these immigration officers have gotten bold, raiding churches, courts, and low-income neighborhoods.
The Immigration and Custom Enforcement Agency, or ICE, insists the raids are targeted, nothing new, and denies thousands of reports across the country.
RaidAlert strives to be give individuals the option to be notified of nearby aggressive immigration activity. Whether you're a journalist needing to get real-time information, a politician concerned with the well-being of your constituents, or an undocumented mother of four frightened at the thought of permanent separation.
